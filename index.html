Rebrand note

NeoIgnite started as one thesis. It had to evolve when bNEO disappeared.

The original NeoIgnite direction was built around a treasury-backed, bNEO-linked token concept. Once bNEO stopped being a reliable long-term base, that route no longer made sense to keep forcing. Rather than dragging a broken structure forward, NeoIgnite was reworked into a Labs identity focused on shipping real Neo-native products.

From protocol thesis to Labs studio
Original NeoIgnite

A reserve-backed product thesis.

The first NeoIgnite concept centered on a treasury-backed design with bNEO as a key part of the structure and long-term economic logic.

Why it stopped

bNEO falling away broke the foundation.

When bNEO stopped being a dependable primitive, the original path lost the base it depended on. That made continuing the old NeoIgnite version the wrong move.

NeoIgnite now

A Labs brand shipping on Neo.

NeoIgnite is now the umbrella Labs identity behind launches like NeoMeme and AgentPay, built to keep creating new Neo-native systems instead of staying locked to one failed thesis.

The thesis

NeoIgnite is not one product. It is the lab that keeps inventing on Neo.

Think of NeoIgnite as a Labs team: a studio that researches, builds, launches, and sharpens new products on Neo. We are here to experiment faster, make stronger product surfaces, and help Neo grow through things that feel current, useful, and culturally alive.

01

02

We treat every launch as part of a broader studio engine. One system informs the next. One product sharpens the lab. One success expands what Neo can support.
